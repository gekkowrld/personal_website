My Childhood

My journey through childhood has been a fascinating exploration of diverse interests. Initially drawn to the allure of the medical field (a passion that still resonates with me), my world expanded in 2018 when I stumbled upon the captivating realm of computers.

Reflecting on my early years, I realize I grew up school-hopping, a unique experience that exposed me to various perspectives and helped shape my life's trajectory.

Interacting with different people along the way provided invaluable guidance, and one quote that particularly resonates with me is:

"As long as it feels good, do it."

Among the myriad of memories from my childhood, one stands out vividly - the time when I excelled in primary school, emerging as the best student in my class.

These formative experiences have left an indelible mark on my character, influencing my interests and aspirations.
